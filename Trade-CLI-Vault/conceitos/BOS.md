# Break of Structure (BOS)

## Definition

**Break of Structure (BOS)** é quando o preço viola a estrutura local estabelecida (swing high ou swing low) anterior ao market maker em [[order-block]] zones. É um sinal de mudança de bias e de potencial continuação.

## Key Characteristics

- **Uptrend BOS:** Price breaks above previous swing high (PHH — Previous Higher High)
- **Downtrend BOS:** Price breaks below previous swing low (PLL — Previous Lower Low)
- **Confirmation:** BOS + retest of break = strength of structure change
- **Timeframe:** Múltiplos timeframes (H4 BOS > H1 BOS > M15 BOS em confluência)

## Types of BOS

### Liquidity Run BOS
- Price breaks structure then pulls back to liquidity zone (e.g., previous LL on way up)
- Market maker enters short after liquidity hunt
- Often precedes reversal within 4–8 bars

### Trend Continuation BOS
- Price breaks structure in direction of trend
- No reversal — continues in trend direction
- Signal of trend strength and acceleration

## When to Trade

✅ **BOS + Order Block confluence** → High confluence setup
✅ **BOS with [[displacement]]** → Trend acceleration signal
✅ **Multiple timeframe BOS** (H4, H1, M15 aligned) → Premium setup

❌ **Isolated BOS without context** → Low confidence
❌ **BOS inside consolidation** → False break likely
❌ **BOS after [[FVG]] — often retraces** → Wait for confirmation

## Risk Rules

1. **Entry:** After BOS + retest of structure
2. **Stop:** Beyond recent [[liquidity-sweep]] or structure extreme
3. **Invalidation:** Break of opposite structure (trend reversal)

## Example: EURUSD H1

```
[PHH at 1.0900 — H4 bears trapped]
    ↑
    BOS above 1.0900 → New HTF uptrend
    ↑
[M15 sellers trapped, pull back for liquidity]
    ↑
[[order-block]] at 1.0880–1.0890 (M15)
    ↑
Entry: 1.0885 (inside ob) + RR 1:3
```

## Connection to ICT

[[metodos/ICT]] relies heavily on BOS as primary entry trigger:
- BOS above market maker [[order-block]]
- Followed by liquidity cleanup
- Retest of break = entry confirmation
